Getting Started

Set Up in Under 5 Minutes

No account registration required. Use your existing Google and Facebook credentials.

1

Open Data Studio and create a new data source

Go to datastudio.google.com, click Create → Data Source, then search for "Facebook Ads Analytics (LQ Analytics)".

2

Authorize with your Facebook account

Click Authorize and log in with your Facebook account. Grant the ads_read and ads_management permissions when prompted. Access is read-only.

3

Select your Ad Account

The connector will automatically load all Business Portfolios and active Ad Accounts your Facebook account has access to. Select the one you want to report on.

4

Connect and start building dashboards

Click Connect. All 60+ metrics and dimensions are now available to use in any Data Studio report, chart, or dashboard. Use Data Studio's date range controls to filter your data.
