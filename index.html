How to Use the Connector

  1. 1 Open Looker Studio: Navigate to your Looker Studio dashboard to begin.
  2. 2 Add Data Source: Click the “Create” button and select “Data Source”.
  3. 3 Locate Connector: Search for and select Facebook Ads (LQ Digital) under the Partner Connectors section.
  4. 4 Secure Authorization: Authorize your Google account and Facebook Ads Business account to enable data flow.
  5. 5 Build Your Report: Select your specific ad accounts and start building your custom dashboard.

Requirements

To use this connector, you must have an active Facebook Ads account with the necessary administrative or analyst permissions to retrieve data. Ensure your Google account has sufficient permissions to create and manage data sources in Looker Studio.
