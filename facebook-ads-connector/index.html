Facebook Ads Connector

The LQ Analytics Facebook Ads Connector lets you securely bring your Facebook (Meta) Ads performance data directly into Looker Studio to create powerful custom dashboards and reports.

Key Features

  • Real-time & historical data
  • Metrics: Impressions, ROAS, CPC, Spend
  • Multi-account & Campaign support
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 Auth
  • Safe, Read-only access

Requirements

  • Google account (Looker Studio)
  • Facebook Ads Account access
  • Ad performance permissions
No Separate Account Required: You do not need to create an account on our website. Simply authorize using your existing Google and Facebook credentials during setup.

How to Set Up

  1. Open Looker Studio
  2. Click Add Data → Data Source
  3. Search for "Facebook Ads Analytics (LQ Analytics)"
  4. Sign in with your Google account
  5. Authorize your Facebook Business Manager or Ad Account
  6. Select the Ad Account(s) you want to connect
  7. Click Connect and start visualizing
