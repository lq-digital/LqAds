Support

If you need help using our connector or services, please review the information below or contact us.

Getting Started

  1. Open Looker Studio
  2. Select “Add Data Source”
  3. Choose the LQ Analytics connector
  4. Authenticate your account
  5. Select your data and create your report

Common Issues

  • Authentication errors: Ensure you are logged into the correct account
  • No data loading: Check your API permissions and data source connection
  • Access issues: Verify account permissions and credentials

Connector Help

If the connector fails to load or return data:

  • Re-authenticate your account
  • Check if your data source is active
  • Ensure you have granted the required permissions
