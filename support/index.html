Support Center

How can we help you today? Review our quick-start guide or troubleshoot common connector issues.

Getting Started

  1. Open Looker Studio
  2. Select “Add Data Source”
  3. Choose the LQ Analytics connector
  4. Authenticate your account
  5. Select your data and create your report

Common Issues

Authentication errors Ensure you are logged into the correct Google and Meta accounts.
No data loading Check your API permissions and ensure the selected Ad Account has active data.
Access issues Verify that your user role in Facebook Business Manager has "View Performance" access.

Connector Troubleshooting

If the connector fails to load or return data, try these steps:

  • Re-authenticate your account within the Looker Studio data source settings.
  • Check if your data source is active and not disabled by a manager.
  • Ensure you have granted all requested permissions during the OAuth popup.
