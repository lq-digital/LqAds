2. Description of Service

LQ Analytics provides the Facebook Ads Analytics Connector, a community connector for Google Data Studio (also known as Looker Studio) that allows users to import Facebook Ads performance data directly into Data Studio for the purpose of building custom reports and dashboards.

The Connector is developed and maintained by LangQuang Digital (LQ Analytics), based in Brisbane, Australia.

3. Use of the Connector

This section specifically covers the Facebook Ads Analytics Connector for Data Studio — what it does, what it accesses, and the conditions of use.

What the Connector Does

The Connector authenticates with your Facebook account via OAuth 2.0 and fetches ad performance data from the Facebook Marketing API (v22.0) on your behalf. This data is passed directly to Data Studio for visualization. The Connector operates at the ad, ad set, and campaign level and supports 60+ metrics and dimensions including impressions, clicks, spend, reach, video analytics, cost-per-action breakdowns, and demographic data.

What the Connector Does Not Do

  • The Connector cannot create, edit, pause, or delete any ads, campaigns, ad sets, or budgets in your Facebook Ads account.
  • The Connector does not store your Facebook ad performance data on LQ Analytics servers.
  • The Connector does not share your data with any third parties beyond what is required to operate (Facebook API and Google Data Studio).

No Account Required

You do not need to create an LQ Analytics account to use the Connector. No registration, subscription, or payment is required. The Connector is provided free of charge.

4. OAuth Authorization and Permissions

To use the Connector, you must authorize it with your Facebook account. By doing so, you grant the Connector the following read-only OAuth 2.0 permissions:

  • ads_read — to read ad performance metrics from your ad accounts
  • ads_management — to enumerate your ad accounts so you can select which to connect
  • business_management — to list Business Portfolios associated with your Facebook profile

All permissions are read-only. You agree to grant only the permissions necessary for the Connector to function. You may revoke these permissions at any time via Facebook Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites, or via the Data Studio data source settings.

Token Storage

Your Facebook OAuth access token is stored securely in Google Apps Script's UserProperties service, scoped to your individual Google account. It is not stored on LQ Analytics servers and is not accessible to LQ Analytics staff. The token is used solely to authenticate API requests to Facebook on your behalf when Data Studio refreshes data.

5. Data Access and No Storage Policy

LQ Analytics does not store, log, cache, or retain any Facebook Ads performance data (campaign names, impressions, spend, conversions, or any other metrics) on its own infrastructure. All ad data fetched from the Facebook Marketing API is returned directly to Data Studio in real time and is not retained by the Connector beyond the duration of the API request.

You are responsible for the data that is displayed within your Data Studio reports and dashboards. LQ Analytics is not responsible for how you use, share, or distribute reports created using the Connector.
